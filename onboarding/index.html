System requirements

  • Windows 10 or 11 and Chrome, Edge, or Vivaldi 116+
  • An NVIDIA GTX/RTX GPU with a working NVIDIA driver
  • At least 5 GB of free disk space

What data leaves this computer?

Transcription, temporary audio, and caption caches stay local. Installation downloads tools and models from public release sources; Any Subtitle has no cloud ASR or telemetry.
